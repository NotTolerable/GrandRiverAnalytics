About Us

Grand River Analytics is a student-run financial publishing website, focused on showcasing qualitative techniques through equity reports. The goal is to provide an outlet to clearly display our technical analysis skills to future employers or those similarly interested in the topic.

The name Grand River Analytics originates from the Grand River, a river which flows through both the universities of Waterloo and Laurier, the place of study for both members of the team.

We currently have two members—seen on the Team page—focused on keeping the biweekly deadline of reports.
